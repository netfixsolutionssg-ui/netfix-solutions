Meet the Founder

Built from real industrial coordination experience.

Netfix Solutions was founded with one clear purpose: to make industrial sourcing and procurement more efficient, transparent and practical for businesses in Singapore.

Through his experience in facilities and project coordination, Rail saw how much time companies can spend identifying suitable vendors, gathering quotations and managing communication across multiple parties.

He established Netfix Solutions to provide a more coordinated approach—helping businesses connect with suitable partners, structure RFQs and review quotations while retaining control over their final procurement decisions.

Rail's long-term vision is to develop Netfix Solutions into a technology-enabled procurement ecosystem supported by AI-assisted vendor matching, quotation comparison and workflow tools.

“We do not just find vendors. We help businesses connect with the right partners for the right requirement.”

Frequently Asked Questions

What businesses usually want to know.

Does Netfix Solutions award the project?

No. The client retains control of vendor evaluation, approval, purchase order placement and final award.

Does Netfix guarantee the lowest quotation?

No. Our role is to support sourcing and quotation coordination. The lowest price may not always represent the most suitable overall offer.

Can Netfix source specialist or overseas manufacturers?

Yes. Depending on the requirement, we can explore specialist suppliers and overseas manufacturing options, including China-based manufacturers.

How can a vendor join the Netfix network?

Vendors can complete the registration form and provide their company details, service categories and capabilities for future opportunity matching.

Is the future AI platform already available?

No. The AI features shown on this website describe Netfix Solutions' future development direction.
